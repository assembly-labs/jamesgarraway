12. Two fractions are equivalent when they represent the same amount, even though they have different numbers. For example, 1/2 and 2/4 both represent half of something. This is important because it lets us describe the same amount in different ways, which is necessary for comparing fractions and adding fractions with different denominators. It also helps us understand that fractions are flexible - there are many ways to name the same amount!
