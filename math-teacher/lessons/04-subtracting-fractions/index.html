Coach's Lesson: Subtracting Fractions with Like Denominators

The WHY: Subtracting fractions is like taking away pieces. If you have 7 slices of pizza and eat 3 slices, you have 4 slices left. The slices didn't shrink - you just have fewer of them. That's why we only subtract the top numbers!

The Rule: When fractions have the same denominator, just subtract the numerators! The denominator stays the same because the SIZE of each piece hasn't changed.

The Method: Step 1: Check that denominators match. Step 2: Subtract the numerators (top - top). Step 3: Keep the denominator the same. Step 4: Simplify if needed.

Football Connection: The Giants need Malik Nabers to cover 7/10 of the field. He's already caught passes for 3/10. How much is left? 7/10 - 3/10 = 4/10 of the field remaining! Coach Brian Daboll knows: the sections didn't change size - they just have fewer yards to cover now!
