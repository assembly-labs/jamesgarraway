The Challenge: Different Denominators

What happens when you try to add 1/2 + 1/4? The pieces are different sizes! We need to make them match first.

Coach's Game Plan:

Step 1: Find a common denominator (a number both fit into)
Step 2: Convert the fractions so they have the same bottom number
Step 3: Now you can add or subtract!
